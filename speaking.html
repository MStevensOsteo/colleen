Speaking & Podcasts

A clear, warm, and credible voice on chronic pain and embodied healing.

Colleen Jorgensen speaks with authority, warmth, and practical clarity on chronic pain, nervous system health, movement, and the language of healing.

What Colleen Speaks On

Topics

Colleen brings nuance, lived insight, and evidence to each of the areas below. These topics work equally well for healthcare audiences and general public listeners.

🪨

Chronic Pain: What It Really Is

Accessible, evidence-based explanations of how chronic pain works — and why the story most people have been told is incomplete.

💡

Pain Science & Hope

What the latest research tells us about pain, recovery, and what is truly possible for people living with persistent symptoms.

⚡

Nervous System & Regulation

How the nervous system learns, amplifies, and holds pain — and what we can do to support greater safety and ease in the body.

🌿

Movement & Embodiment

How movement — done wisely, with awareness — can restore confidence, reduce threat, and rebuild a trusting relationship with the body.

💬

Language, Communication & Permission

The profound impact of how we talk about pain — with ourselves, with practitioners, and in public health messaging.

🎓

Education for Practitioners

How healthcare and movement professionals can bring better pain understanding into their clinical and teaching practice.

Voice & Style

Why audiences connect

Colleen does not lecture. She illuminates. Her conversations move between science and story, between clinical detail and lived experience, in a way that makes complex ideas feel accessible and genuinely hopeful.

Listeners and attendees consistently describe her as someone who makes them feel understood first — and then gently, skillfully changes the way they see the topic. She is direct without being blunt, evidence-based without being dry, and deeply human throughout.
